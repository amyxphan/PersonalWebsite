Get to Know Me!

Hello! My name is Amy Phan and I'm a student majoring in Computer Engineering at the University of North Texas. I am the President and Co-Founder of the Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers at UNT (SASE UNT). My interest in engineering began as a little girl. Rather than playing dress up with dolls, I loved to break them about and unscrew toy cars to learn how to put everything back together. As I grew older I implemented these habits into my day to day life. I am an aspiring software engineer passionate about website and app development.
